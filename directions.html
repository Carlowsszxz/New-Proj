Complete instructions for using the RFID Login System

Step 1: Creating Your Account

1Go to the Sign Up Page

On the login page, you'll see a "Sign up" link below the login form. Click it to access the registration form.

2Fill Out Your Information

Enter the following required information:

  • Email: Your valid email address (this will be used for login)
  • First Name: Your first name
  • Last Name: Your last name
  • Password: A secure password (remember this for future logins)

3Create Your Account

Click the "Create Account" button to submit your registration.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: After creating your account, check your email inbox for a confirmation email. You must confirm your email address before you can log in.

Step 2: Logging In

1Enter Your Credentials

On the login page, enter:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your password

2Click Login

Click the "Login" button. If your credentials are correct and your email is confirmed, you'll be redirected to your Dashboard.

‚ùó If login fails: Make sure you've confirmed your email address. Check your spam folder if you didn't receive the confirmation email.

Step 3: Setting Up Your RFID Card

1Access the RFID Reader

Go to the physical RFID reader at your table/lab location.

2Tap Your RFID Card

Tap your RFID card on the reader. The LCD screen will display your unique RFID Card ID (it will look something like: 93B12CDA).

üìù Write it down: The RFID ID will be displayed on the LCD screen. Make sure to copy it exactly as shown (it's case-sensitive!).

3Assign Your RFID Card in Dashboard

After logging into your Dashboard, you'll see an "Assign Your RFID Card" section. Follow these steps:

  • Enter the exact RFID Card ID you copied from the LCD screen
  • Make sure to copy-paste it exactly (it's case-sensitive)
  • Click "Assign RFID Card"
‚ö†Ô∏è Note: You only need to do this once. After assignment, you can use your RFID card to log in and out at the physical reader.

Step 4: Using Your Dashboard

The Dashboard is your main hub where you can see your current status and navigate to other sections.

1Navigation Bar

At the top of the page, you'll find a navigation bar with three options:

  • Dashboard: Your main page (current page)
  • Seat Map: View available seats
  • Reports: Submit reports or issues
  • Logout: End your session (located on the right)

2Your Status Section

After assigning your RFID card, you'll see:

  • Your Status: Shows whether you're currently logged in via RFID (green) or logged out (red)
  • Your Seat: Shows which seat you're currently occupying (e.g., "Table 1, Seat 2 - OCCUPIED")
  • Current Noise Level: Displays the real-time noise level at your table

3Using RFID at the Physical Reader

Once your RFID card is assigned:

  • To Log In: Tap your RFID card on the reader when you arrive
  • To Log Out: Tap your RFID card again when you leave
  • The system will automatically assign you an available seat
  • Your status on the Dashboard will update in real-time

Step 5: Viewing Seat Map

1Access the Seat Map

Click "Seat Map" in the navigation bar to see the current occupancy status of all seats.

2Understanding the Map

  • üü¢ Green (Occupied): Seats that are currently taken
  • ‚ö´ Gray (Available): Seats that are free

3View Full Map

Click the "üó∫Ô∏è View Full Map" button to see the complete floor plan in a larger view.

The map refreshes automatically every 5 seconds to show the latest seat availability.

Step 6: Submitting Reports

1Navigate to Reports

Click "Reports" in the navigation bar to access the reports section.

2Submit a Report

Fill out the report form:

  • Report Type: Select "Issue", "Suggestion", or "Feedback"
  • Title: Give your report a brief title
  • Description: Provide detailed information about your report

Click "Submit Report" when done.

3View Your Reports

Scroll down to see "My Submitted Reports" where you can:

  • View all reports you've submitted
  • See the status of each report (Pending, Reviewing, Resolved)
  • Read admin replies if your report has been responded to

Step 7: Logging Out

1Always Use the Logout Button

When you're finished, click the "Logout" button in the top-right corner of the navigation bar.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: You must use the Logout button to properly end your session. Simply closing the browser tab or navigating away is not recommended and may show a warning message.

2RFID Logout

If you're logged in via RFID at the physical reader, make sure to tap your card again before leaving to log out and free up your seat.

üí° Helpful Tips & Important Notes

‚úÖ Stay Within Student Pages

You can freely navigate between Dashboard, Seat Map, and Reports. The system will prevent you from accessing admin pages or returning to login without logging out.

üìß Email Confirmation

Always check your email after registration. You cannot log in until you confirm your email address via the link sent to you.

üîê RFID Card ID

Your RFID Card ID is case-sensitive! Make sure to copy it exactly as shown on the LCD screen, or use copy-paste for accuracy.

ü™ë Automatic Seat Assignment

When you tap your RFID card to log in, the system automatically assigns you the first available seat. You don't need to choose.

üîî Noise Level Monitoring

The system monitors noise levels in real-time. If noise gets too high, warnings will appear on the LCD screen at your table.

üö™ Proper Logout

Always use the Logout button. This ensures your seat is freed up for other students and your session is properly ended.

‚ùì Need Help? If you encounter any issues or have questions, contact your lab administrator or submit a report through the Reports section.
